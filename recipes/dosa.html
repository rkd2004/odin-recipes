Dosa

Description

A South Indian savory crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Add salt to the batter and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladleful of batter, spread into a thin circle, and drizzle oil around the edges.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
